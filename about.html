Best Travel and Tour Experience

Traveling isn’t just about reaching a destination; it’s about embracing the journey, creating memories, and experiencing the beauty of new horizons. At Best Travel and Tour Experience, we understand that every traveler seeks something extraordinary, whether it’s the thrill of adventure, the tranquility of nature, or the immersion into vibrant cultures.

Key attractions include the Blue Nile Falls, ancient monasteries, and the serene Lake Tana, the source of the Blue Nile River. Whether you're looking for adventure, history, or relaxation, Bahir Dar has it all.


Why Visit Bahir Dar?

Stunning natural scenery
Rich cultural heritage
Historic monasteries and churches
Friendly locals and vibrant markets


The palm-fringed university town of Bahir Dar nestles at the southern tip of the vast expanse of water that is Lake Tana, Ethiopia’s largest lake. Its waters stretch over the horizon, more than 80km to the north. On arrival, it’s not hard to imagine why Emperor Haile Sellasie built a modest palace here, even toying with the idea of moving his country’s capital to this very place.
